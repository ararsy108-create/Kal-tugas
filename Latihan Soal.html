A. Hitung Determinan berikut dengan menggunakan ekspansi baris \[det(A) = \sum_{k=1}^{n} (-1)^{i+k} \, a_{ik} \, M_{ik}\]
\[A_{ij} \text{ adalah submatriks yang diperoleh dengan menghapus baris } i \text{ dan kolom } j \text{ dari matriks } A_{m \times n}, \text{ dengan } 1 \leq i, j \leq n\]
\[M_{ij} = \det(A_{ij})\]

SOAL

1.     \[A = \begin{bmatrix} -7 & -5 \\ 1 & 4 \end{bmatrix}\]

2.     \[A = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 2 & -3 \\ 1 & -2 & -1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \]

3.     \[A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -3 & 1 & 1 \\ -3 & 1 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & -3 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 & -3 \end{bmatrix} \]

A.Perhitungan Determinan dengan Ekspansi Kofaktor Baris
Menggunakan rumus: \[det(A)=\sum_{k=1}^{n}(-1)^{i+k} \, a_{ik} \, \det(A_{ik})\] 1. Matriks 2×2 \[A=\begin{bmatrix} -7 & -5\\ 1 & 4 \end{bmatrix}\] Ambil baris pertama $(i=1)$: \[det(A)=(-1)^{1+1}(-7)\begin{vmatrix}4\end{vmatrix} +(-1)^{1+2}(-5)\begin{vmatrix}1\end{vmatrix}\] \[= (+)(-7)(4) + (-)(-5)(1)\] \[= -28 + 5 = -23\]
Hasil: \[det(A) = -23\] 2. Matriks 3×3 \[ A=\begin{bmatrix} 0 & 2 & -3\\ 1 & -2 & -1\\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \]
Ekspansi baris pertama: \[ \det(A)= (-1)^{1+1}(0)\begin{vmatrix}-2 & -1\\0 & 1\end{vmatrix} +(-1)^{1+2}(2)\begin{vmatrix}1 & -1\\0 & 1\end{vmatrix} +(-1)^{1+3}(-3)\begin{vmatrix}1 & -2\\0 & 0\end{vmatrix} \]
Minor 1 \[\begin{vmatrix}-2 & -1\\0 & 1\end{vmatrix} = -2\] Dikalikan 0 → hasil 0

Minor 2 \[ \begin{vmatrix}1 & -1\\0 & 1\end{vmatrix}=1 \] \[ (-1)^{3}=-1 \Rightarrow - (2)(1)=-2 \]
Minor 3 \[ \begin{vmatrix}1 & -2\\0 & 0\end{vmatrix}=0 \]
Hasil akhir \[ \det(A)=0 - 2 + 0 = -2 \]
Hasil: \[ \det(A) = -2 \]
3. Matriks 4×4 \[ A=\begin{bmatrix} 1 & -3 & 1 & 1\\ -3 & 1 & 1 & 1\\ 1 & 1 & -3 & 1\\ 1 & 1 & 1 & -3 \end{bmatrix} \]
Ekspansi baris pertama: \[ \det(A)= (+1)\begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1 & 1\\ 1 & -3 & 1\\ 1 & 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix} -(-3)\begin{vmatrix} -3 & 1 & 1\\ 1 & -3 & 1\\ 1 & 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix} +(1)\begin{vmatrix} -3 & 1 & 1\\ 1 & 1 & 1\\ 1 & 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix} -(1)\begin{vmatrix} -3 & 1 & 1\\ 1 & 1 & -3\\ 1 & 1 & 1 \end{vmatrix} \]
Perhitungan Minor Minor 1 \[ = 16 \] Minor 2 \[ = -16 \] Minor 3 \[ = 16 \] Minor 4 \[ = -16 \]
Substitusi \[ \det(A)= (1)(16) + (3)(-16) + (1)(16) - (1)(-16) \] \[ =16 - 48 +16 +16 = 0 \]
Kesimpulan

1. \[det(A) = -23\]
2. \[det(A) = -2\]

B. Gunakan rumus matriks adjoin untuk menghitung invers dari matriks berikut: \[ (\text{adj } A)_{ij} = (-1)^{i+j} M_{ji} \] dan \[ A^{-1} = \frac{1}{\det A} \, \text{adj } A \]
1. Matriks \[ A = \begin{bmatrix} -7 & -5 \\ 1 & 4 \end{bmatrix} \]
Determinan \[ \det(A) = (-7)(4) - (-5)(1) = -28 + 5 = -23 \]
Adjoin (adj A) Untuk matriks \(2 \times 2\): \[ \text{adj}(A) = \begin{bmatrix} d & -b \\ -c & a \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 4 & 5 \\ -1 & -7 \end{bmatrix} \]
Invers \[ A^{-1} = \frac{1}{-23} \begin{bmatrix} 4 & 5 \\ -1 & -7 \end{bmatrix} \]
2. Matriks \[ A = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 2 & -3 \\ 1 & -2 & -1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \]
Determinan Karena matriks segitiga atas: \[ \det(A) = 0 \cdot (-2) \cdot 1 = 0 \]
Kesimpulan \[ \det(A) = 0 \Rightarrow A^{-1} \text{ tidak ada} \]
3. Matriks \[ A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -3 & 1 & 1 \\ -3 & 1 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & -3 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 & -3 \end{bmatrix} \]
Determinan \[ \det(A) = 0 \]
Kesimpulan \[ \det(A) = 0 \Rightarrow A^{-1} \text{ tidak ada} \]
